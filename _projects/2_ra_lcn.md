Retain-aware latent concept nulling for generative models: a single projection at inference removes a target concept while explicitly preserving retained knowledge via Mahalanobis geometry. No retraining, no gradient access. Validated on GPT-2 (text-to-text) and Stable Diffusion v1.5 (text-to-image and image-to-image). Manuscript in preparation, with Austin Fu.

Code: [github.com/AustinAllen/RA-LCN-on-Machine-Unlearning](https://github.com/AustinAllen/RA-LCN-on-Machine-Unlearning)
