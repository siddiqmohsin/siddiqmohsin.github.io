A research platform for multi-perspective analysis of open-source geopolitical commentary: 38 author-specific RAG corpora (~66,000 chunks) convened as an analyst panel, with a corpus-derived stance-aware knowledge graph, provenance-gated retrieval, threshold-bounded autonomous monitoring, and a claim-level evidence register resolving into per-analyst calibration scorecards. Runs live on local open-source models.
